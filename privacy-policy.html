Privacy Policy for ScanVault

Last updated: October 26, 2025

1. Introduction

ScanVault ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

ScanVault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data processed by the app remains on your device.

3. Camera and Photo Library Permission

ScanVault requires camera access to scan documents and photo library access to save and import images. Images captured or selected are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, share, or transmit your scanned documents to any external servers.

4. Storage Permission

The app requires storage permission to save scanned documents as PDF files on your device. All documents remain under your control on your local device storage.

5. Advertising (Free Version)

The free version of ScanVault displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process the following data for advertising purposes:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and app usage information
  • General location information

This information is used to show personalized ads and measure ad performance. For more information about how Google processes this data, please refer to:

  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google's Advertising Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings.

6. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

ScanVault offers premium subscriptions through Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and Google respectively. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription details are managed through your App Store or Google Play account.

7. Data Security

Since all your documents are stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your data. We recommend securing your device with appropriate security measures such as device passcode, biometric authentication, or encryption.

8. Third-Party Services

ScanVault uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements (free version only)
  • Google ML Kit - For text recognition (OCR), processed entirely on-device
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store - For in-app purchase processing

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is not directed at children under 13.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on external servers, all your data remains on your device under your control. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
