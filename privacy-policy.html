Privacy Policy for ScanVault

Last updated: October 13, 2025

1. Introduction

ScanVault ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

ScanVault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data processed by the app remains on your device.

3. Camera Permission

ScanVault requires camera access to scan documents. Images captured are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, share, or transmit your scanned documents to any external servers.

4. Storage Permission

The app requires storage permission to save scanned documents as PDF files on your device. All documents remain under your control on your local device storage.

5. Advertising

ScanVault displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

Since all your documents are stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your data. We recommend securing your device with appropriate security measures.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
